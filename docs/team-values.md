# Team Values

- **Communicate in the open** — kind while honest, assume good intent, no sacred cows
- **Drive work end-to-end** — don't stop at boundaries, own it across layers
- **Unblock each other first** — enable peers to succeed, review quickly, share knowledge
- **Reliability is the most important feature** — operations mindset, lean implementations, minimize maintenance surface
- **Run through two-way doors, deliberate before one-way doors**
- **Solid foundations, fast iterations**
